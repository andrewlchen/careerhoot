IntroKick FAQs and Terms of Service

  • What is IntroKick? IntroKick is the best tool to find warm leads through people you already know.
  • Who built it? It was built by this guy.
  • How does it work? When you login with LinkedIn and join an IntroKick group, we'll show you the LinkedIn contacts of everyone in that group (besides you) that you're NOT connected to. You can sort and find potential leads by company or industry. Then, if you see someone you want to contact, you can ask for an intro from folks in that group who are connected to that person (which we highlight to you). So even if you don't know exactly who you're looking for -- you just know you want to meet people at company X or in industry Y -- we show you targeted lists of those people for you to quickly get introduced to them. Pure awesome.
  • How do you use my LinkedIn data? We use your data only to show you potential leads. We never contact any of your connections, we don't solicit them for anything, and we don't spam you, ever. The only times we email you are when someone requests to join a group you're part of specifically from you, or when you invite someone to join a group. Other than that, you won't hear from us.
  • Why should I subscribe? Well, the biggest reason (I hope) is that you find IntroKick useful. But we also rely solely on support from our users to keep the site running. No ads, no revenue from companies, nothing. Just you guys. So if you like what we're doing, please consider subscribing to help support us.
  • How much does it cost and how do I subscribe? IntroKick is free for the first 14 days, then just $4.95 per month thereafter. To subscribe, simply login, then click the "Open Sesame" link in the upper right.
  • Refunds? Unfortunately we cannot provide refunds. Even if you forgot to cancel and it's only been one day into your new billing cycle. We do this because it's very manual to process refunds for purchases this low. After all, it's only $4.95 per month. So if you forgot to cancel and got charged an extra $4.95 this month, then just email me and we'll cancel you immediately so you don't get charged again. But please be aware your last $4.95 charge cannot be refunded, even if you meant to cancel but simply forgot to.
  • Canceling your account. Also, please note that your subscription expires immediately after you cancel -- again, even if it's only been one day into your new billing cycle. Again, the reason is because of the administration required to keep track of different "types" of subscribers, i.e., regular subscribers on the one hand, and subscribers who have canceled but technically haven't expired on the other.
  • Feedback, questions, comments? Just email us.
